How To Draw a Smiley Face




Drawing is something that is really important to me. It's something that allows
anyone to express their feelings and be creative. This is why this website will
teach you how to draw

Steps

How to's

Examples

Step 1

Draw a circle, an egg, or any shape that somewhat resembles a circle.

Step 2

Draw 2 dots inside the circle that are next to each other, however, you may draw them whatever way you please and place them wherever depending on the type of face you want to make.

Step 3

Draw a u-shaped line underneath the 2 dots - still inside the circle.

Add details, or don't. It's all up to the what you want to draw and the level you are drawing at.
You can use this drawing tool implemented underneath to draw what you want!
